Odds and Evens

This is a game you play against the computer.
You select odds or evens. Here are a couple of cases to consider.

Case 1:
If you select odds, then the computer has evens.
You select either 1 or 0 and the computer does the same.
Program adds your number and the computer's number together.
If the sum of the two numbers is odd then you win.
If the sum of the two numbers is even then the computer wins.

Case 2:
If you select evens, then the computer has odds.
You select either 1 or 0 and the computer does the same.
Program adds your number and the computer's number together.
If the sum of the two numbers is even then you win.
If the sum of the two numbers is odd then the computer wins.
